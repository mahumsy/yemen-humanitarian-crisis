Take Action

Give hope to these children and families.

There are many ways that you can make a difference since a little can go a long way. Start by learning the facts. There are various donation links listed on this website, as well as petitions. Petitions take no more than 2 minutes to sign. Ad revenue from certain apps will go towards the cause as well. Bringing awareness to this issue allows more people to be educated about the situation, and will make this issue a worldwide concern. Public outcry and increased media coverage can persuade leaders to help these people who are in need.
