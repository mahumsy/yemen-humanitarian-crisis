About the Creator of this Website

Mahum Syed

Hi there!

My name is Mahum Syed and I created this informational website during my 2020 summer program with Girls Who Code. I based it off of the humanitarian crisis that has been going on in Yemen because I think it's very important that people know about it and find out how they can help. All of the resources, including donation sites, petition sites, links to apps, YouTube videos, and more can be found on this page.

Using my resources and knowledge gained through the program, I coded this website using HTML, CSS, and Javascript. There are some interactive elements, such as the checklist, and there are also functional links that lead to other sites.

My second project is a self-checker for COVID-19 symptoms, which can be found at this link : COVID-19 Self-Checker

I am currently a second-year student at the University of Southern California as a computer science and business administration major. I am developing my coding skills and currently learning C++ ! You can find my LinkedIn and resume here.
